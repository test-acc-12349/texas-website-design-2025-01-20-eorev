Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

Most projects are completed within 2-4 weeks. Simple websites can be done in as little as 1 week, while more complex projects may take 6-8 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What's included in the free hosting package?

Our comprehensive hosting package includes SSL security, daily backups, 24/7 monitoring, CDN integration, unlimited bandwidth, and regular security updates. We manage everything so you can focus on your business.

Do you offer ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes, we provide dedicated support and maintenance packages starting at $99/month, including regular updates, security patches, content updates, and technical support with a guaranteed 24-hour response time.

What is your pricing structure?

We offer transparent, fixed-fee pricing based on project scope. Basic websites start at $2,999, while e-commerce solutions begin at $4,999. All prices include hosting, SSL, and basic SEO optimization.

Can you help with content creation?

Yes, we offer professional copywriting and content creation services. Our team can help develop engaging, SEO-optimized content that resonates with your target audience and drives conversions.
